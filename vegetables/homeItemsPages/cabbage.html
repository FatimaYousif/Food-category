About this item

The group of plants called cabbages includes a number of familiar vegetables. Among them are head cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower, brussels sprouts, kale, and kohlrabi. All kinds of cabbage developed from the same plant, called wild cabbage. They are grown in mild climates throughout the world. Cabbage plants have leaves with a waxy coating. This coating usually gives the leaf a gray-green or blue-green color.
