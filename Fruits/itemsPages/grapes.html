Grapes

Grapes are bulb-shaped fruits. They can be eaten fresh, dried to make raisins, or crushed to make grape juice or wine. There are more than 8,000 varieties of grape. Grapes grow in wooded and warm regions of the world. Large quantities grow in France, Spain, Italy, Turkey, and Georgia. About a thousand varieties of grapes have been developed in the United States.
