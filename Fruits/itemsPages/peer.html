Pear

Pears are a popular fruit that can be dried, cooked, or eaten fresh. People use pears in salads, desserts, and jams. They also drink pear juice. Pears are a good source of potassium and fiber. Pears grow in mild regions throughout the world. China, Italy, and the United States are the countries that grow the most pears. Among U.S. states, Washington, Oregon, and California usually grow the largest amount of pears.
